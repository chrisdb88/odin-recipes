Brick-Oven Pizza

(This page will not actually cover the in-depth instructions required to prepare this dish and is merely for demonstration puproses. See link below for actual instructions on original page.)

In-depth Instructions

Description

This simple brick-oven pizza recipe has been made famous by several well-known, Brooklyn wood-fire pizzerias. Best accompanied by ice-cold, pilsner-style lager beer.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Toppings
  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 ounces low moisture mozzarella cheese, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup no salt added canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 leaves fresh basil, torn

Steps

  1. Make the dough.
  2. Knead dough.
  3. Divide dough ball into two dough balls.
  4. Refrigerate for 16 hours.
  5. Remove from fridge 1 hour prior to using.
  6. Preheat oven to 550 degrees F.
  7. Stretch dough into pizza shape.
  8. Apply toppings
  9. Bake for 4 to 6 minutes
  10. Enjoy!
